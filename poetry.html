20 January 2025 üå∏

Dil ke har kona mein roshan hai aapse,
Har subah nayi umeed jagti hai aapse,
Zindagi ke safar mein raahat milti hai aapse,
Har gham ki andheri raat dhalkti hai aapse,
Mohabbat ke har rang se sajti hai zindagi aapse,
Meri dhadkan bhi beqarar ho jaati hai aapse.

28 January 2025 üå∏

Tumko chura kar zamana gareeb kar diya,
Mohabbat ka har jazba azeeb kar diya.

Wafa ke safar mein chala main akela,
Teri chahat ne mujhko naseeb kar diya.

Na dekhi kabhi maine duniya ki raah,
Teri yaadon ne dil ko naheeb kar diya.

Har mod pe sirf tera hi naam liya,
Mujhko mohabbat ka tareeb kar diya.

Zamana chaahe jitna bhi badal jaaye,
Tujhse wafa ko lazeeb kar diya.

15 February 2025 üå∏

Husn-e-jana ki tareef mumkin nahi,
Zubaan kahe bhi to alfaaz kam pad jaayein.
Teri aankhon ka noor chand se roshan hai,
Par chand bhi teri roshni mein kho jaaye.

Teri zulfon ki ghata jab hawaon se mile,
To mausam bhi khud se sharmane lage.
Teri hasi ki shararat jo baadalon se kahe,
To barsaat bhi tujhme sukoon pane lage.

Tu bas ek ehsaas nahi, ek poori kahani hai,
Jise likhne baithein to qalam bhi thak jaaye.
Husn-e-jana ki tareef mumkin nahi,
Kyunki tu sirf khoobsurat nahi, ek adaa hai jo har dil chaahe.üíï

05 January 2025 üå∏

Chaand sharmayega chaandni raat mai,
Yu na zulfon ko apni sawara karo.
Teri aankhon ka jaadu chale har jagah,
Yu na palkon ko apni utaara karo.

Tere chehre ki roshni din banegi,
Yu na ghoonghat se isse tu pukaara karo.
Teri baaton mein khushbu hai shaam-o-seher,
Yu na lab se hawaon pe waara karo.

12 January 2025 üå∏

Tera naam likha hai hawaon pe humne,
Yu na khwabon ko apne mitaya karo.
Teri chaahat mein dil bekaraar hai yun,
Yu na dhadkan ko apni sataya karo.

Teri baahon mein aake fiza keh rahi,
Yu na mehka hua din gawaaya karo.
Meri rooh bhi bas tera zikr chahe,
Yu na apni mohabbat chupaya karo.üíï

12 January 2025 üå∏

Aaine ki nazar lag na jaye kahin,
Tera husn bhi chhup na sakega kahin.
Ye chandni bhi sharma na jaye kahin,
Teri zulfon ka saya na chhaye kahin.
Meri dhadkan bhi tham na jaye kahin,
Mohabbat ki aag lag na jaye kahin.
